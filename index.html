Safety Buoy

Emergency procedures and life-saving protocols for all crew and passengers.

Man Overboard Procedure

1. Shout “MAN OVERBOARD!” + port/starboard
2. Throw life ring & deploy flotation device
3. Assign dedicated spotter – never lose visual
4. Activate MOB on GPS/plotter
5. Execute Williamson turn or immediate return maneuver
6. Deploy rescue boat if conditions allow

Fire Response Protocol

1. Sound continuous fire alarm
2. Announce location over PA
3. Attempt to extinguish with nearest extinguisher (if safe)
4. Close doors & vents to contain fire
5. Activate fixed CO₂ or water mist system (if applicable)
6. Prepare for possible evacuation

Medical Emergency

1. Call “MEDICAL EMERGENCY” + location
2. Administer first aid / CPR if trained
3. Retrieve AED and medical kit
4. Contact shore medical advisory service via radio/satellite
5. Prepare for medevac if required

Abandon Ship Procedure

1. Commander orders “ABANDON SHIP”
2. Sound 7 short + 1 long blast
3. Don life jackets
4. Transmit Mayday on VHF Ch. 16 & SSB
5. Launch liferafts & survival suits
6. Take EPIRB, SART, grab bag, and water
7. Stay together and await rescue
